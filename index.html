    How to play?

  • This game is the online version of the Simon electronic game with 4 large illuminated buttons: blue, green, cream and pink.
  • Each key is linked to an electronic sound that will help the player memorize the light key sequence.
  • You must press the buttons pressed by the computer in order. The perfect game to test your image and sound memory. You will love this memory game!
